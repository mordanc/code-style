About Me

My name is Aaron and I'm a Senior at CNU, majoring in Computer Science. Over the years I've collected and written down some common coding practices that I try to follow, and I've always wanted a place that I could store them online. So, I decided to create this website and publicly host it, so that the information is always accessible to myself, and anyone else that might find it useful.

I don't claim to be an expert in code styling or good practice, but my hope is that by writing about it here, I'll learn a lot from researching it.
